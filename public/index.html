Plotly Example

This is an example of using Plotly to graph two different use cases in Ajijic Mexico. The CFE (Mexican Electric Company) sets the rate by adding up the usage in the past 12 months. If it goes over 3000 killowatt hours the rate is about 4.5 times the normal rate. The billing is in 2 month cycles. So this means that it is better keep the average usage below 500 killowatt hours per billing cycle.
